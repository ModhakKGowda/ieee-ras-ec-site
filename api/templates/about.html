ABOUT THE CLUB

Engineering Autonomous Systems

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) at PESU EC Campus is an active engineering hub. We build physical machinery, write real-time control loops, and create space for creative hands-on tinkering. If you want to turn theoretical math and code into moving physical robots, this is your community.

PESU EC Campus Sandbox

Nestled in the heart of Electronic City, the PESU EC Campus provides an elite engineering environment. Surrounded by major global tech clusters, students have access to state-of-the-art lab facilities, embedded circuitry sandboxes, and modern prototyping workshops.

Active Focus Tracks

We specialize in autonomous navigation algorithms, microcontroller programming (ESP32/Arduino), computer vision, and ROS-based kinematics. We bypass simple slide decks to build real, operating hardware stacks.
