IEEE RAS CHAPTER

Advancing Robotics Engineering

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) student branch chapter at PES University, Electronic City Campus is a premier community dedicated to nurturing direct hardware engineering capabilities. We focus on bridging industrial methodologies with creative student innovation, working directly with embedded systems, spatial algorithmic processing, and robotic automation architectures.

PESU EC Campus Ecosystem

Situated directly inside Bangalore's premier IT hub, the Electronic City Campus (ECC) offers an incredible, resource-rich technical sandbox for engineering undergraduates. Our chapter leverages this location to connect students directly with local technical facilities, modern fabrication tools, and robust micro-electronics lab pipelines to support independent prototyping.

Core Technological Tracks

From working with real-time embedded firmware configurations to setting up autonomous vision tracking logic, we break down complex problems into clear building blocks. We arrange structured, peer-to-peer code sprints and physical build labs, helping students create impressive project portfolios for modern industry paths.
